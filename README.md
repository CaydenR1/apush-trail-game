# 🗺️ The APUSH Trail

An *Oregon Trail* style text-based RPG built to help students survive the **AP U.S. History** curriculum and score a 5 on the exam. Playable directly in your web browser via GitHub Pages!

## 🕹️ Core Mechanics

Players navigate through all **9 APUSH Units** (from 1491 to the Present Day). Every checkpoint brings critical historical dilemmas where choices directly impact 5 core national resources:

* 💰 **Money (Treasury):** Funds your infrastructure and military actions.
* 🗳️ **Political Support:** Your popularity. Drop to 0%, and you lose the next election.
* 📦 **Military Supplies:** Food, ammunition, and medicine needed to avoid attrition.
* ⚔️ **Military Power:** Your raw troop strength and combat morale.
* 🇺🇸 **National Unity:** The stability of the Union. Low unity triggers civil war!

---

## 📚 Educational Value

This game translates abstract history concepts into concrete game logic:
* **Cause & Effect:** See how early choices (like the Whiskey Rebellion) alter your resources centuries later.
* **Stimulus Practice:** Recreates the critical thinking needed for APUSH multiple-choice and DBQ questions.
* **Chronological Order:** Follows the exact College Board roadmap across 9 chronological checkpoints.
